Discord Teams Phone Admin Group

There are lots of user groups in IT. I decieded to create an "Admin Group". It's basically the same as a user group but since we're all administrators, we call ourselves an Admin Group. 



Why Discord?

Since this group is all about Teams, why do we use Discord?


Discord is a great way of building and managing a community. Of course we all work with Teams and we love it. Nonetheless, Teams requires a license and an account is always tied to an organization. With Discord, everbody joins using their own personal account. This way, an account will never be tied to an organization.

What is it about?

We discuss stuff about different topics related to Microsoft Teams. We also use this group to ask other members to test/verify certain scenarios, if we're not sure about something.

What topics are discussed?

We have dedicated channels for the following topics:


- General

- Teams Admin Center

- PowerShell

- Session Border Controllers

- Cry for help (if you're stuck solving a particular problem)

- Issue reporting (if you think that the next Teams/M365 outage is on it's way)

- Teams Clients

- Feedback voting (ask other members to support your feedback vote)

- Teams Phones and Teams Rooms

How many of us are there?

We've grown to around 80 people already!

Who are the Admins?

Since @heig and me already spoke frequently on Twitter or Teams about Teams stuff, we decided that having our own Discord Server would be nice. Since @AndresGorzelany has been a very active and supportive member, we've also added him as an admin.
